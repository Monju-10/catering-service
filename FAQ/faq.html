What is a "tasting menu" in a fine dining restaurant?

Answer: A tasting menu is a multi-course meal where guests are served small portions of a variety of dishes, allowing them to experience a range of flavors and dishes created by the chef.

What is the primary focus of a "farm-to-table" restaurant?

Answer: A "farm-to-table" restaurant focuses on serving dishes made from locally sourced, fresh ingredients, often directly from farms to ensure quality and sustainability.

In restaurant terminology, what is a "prix fixe" menu?

Answer: A "prix fixe" menu is a set menu with a fixed price for a multi-course meal, where the customer pays a single price for a pre-determined selection of dishes.

What does the term "al dente" mean when referring to pasta in restaurants?

Answer: "Al dente" means that the pasta is cooked to be firm to the bite, rather than being overly soft or mushy.

What is the role of a sommelier in a restaurant?

Answer: A sommelier is a wine expert responsible for recommending and pairing wines with food, managing the wine list, and providing knowledgeable service to enhance the dining experience.
